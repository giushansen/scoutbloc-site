Frequently Asked Questions

Can I scan multiple applications?

Yes! Each application requires its own subscription at $99/month. Contact us for volume discounts on 5+ applications.

What happens if you find a vulnerability?

You'll receive an immediate alert via email (and Slack if configured). The report includes severity rating, detailed description, and step-by-step remediation guidance.

Do you offer manual penetration testing?

Our $99/month plan uses automated DAST scanning. For manual VAPT with human security experts, please contact us for custom pricing.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We're based in Singapore and comply with PDPA regulations. We only scan your application - we don't store sensitive data. All reports are encrypted and access-controlled.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, there are no contracts or commitments. Cancel anytime from your dashboard and you won't be charged again.
